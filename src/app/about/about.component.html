Passion

We love teaching karate, but none of our instructors do it for a living. Unlike commercialized schools that are focused on profit, we prefer to invest the time and ensure you learn to apply effective techniques.

Values

Friendship and cooperation are at the core of our values. Okinawan karate was born out of a necessity to defend against life-threatening danger. A mature attitude is required to safely practice techniques which are illegal in conventional sport karate styles.

Experience

As students of Sensei Iha, our instructors each have at least 30 years experience practicing and teaching Okinawan Karate. As a newcomer, you will receive one-on-one instruction as a part of every class.

Authentic

We are one of the few schools in the country to maintain unbroken lineage. You will learn authentic techniques for true self defense, not for winning points in a tournament.
